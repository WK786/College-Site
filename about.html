About the Institute

NIT Polytechnic, Nagpur operates under Shri Sai Shikshan Sanstha with the objective of providing quality technical education. The institute aims to develop skilled diploma engineers capable of meeting industrial and societal needs.

Vision

To be a leading polytechnic institute fostering technical excellence and innovation.

Mission

  • Deliver quality diploma education.
  • Promote practical and industry-oriented learning.
  • Encourage ethical and professional values.
