Admission Details

Admissions are conducted through the Centralized Admission Process (CAP) as per Directorate of Technical Education (DTE) norms.

Eligibility

Candidate must have passed SSC (10th) examination from a recognized board.

Required Documents

  • SSC Marksheet
  • Leaving Certificate
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • CAP Allotment Letter
