Diploma Engineering Programs

Computer Engineering

The Computer Engineering department focuses on programming, database management, networking, web technologies, and emerging IT trends. Students receive strong practical exposure through modern computer laboratories.

Mechanical Engineering

This department provides in-depth knowledge in manufacturing processes, machine design, thermal engineering, and industrial applications supported by well-equipped workshops.

Civil Engineering

Civil Engineering emphasizes construction technology, surveying, structural design, and infrastructure development with practical site-based learning.

Electrical Engineering

The department covers electrical machines, power systems, control systems, and industrial automation, preparing students for core technical industries.
