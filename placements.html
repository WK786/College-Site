About Placement Cell

The Training and Placement Cell at NIT Polytechnic plays a vital role in bridging the gap between students and industry. It organizes campus drives, industrial visits, skill development workshops, and personality development programs.

Placement Support

  • Pre-placement training sessions
  • Mock interviews & aptitude preparation
  • Industry interaction programs
  • Internship opportunities

Recruitment Opportunities

Students are placed in various industries, manufacturing units, IT companies, and infrastructure firms through campus recruitment drives.
